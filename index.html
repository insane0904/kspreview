Authentic Kolhapuri Flavors, Served with Love

At Kolhapur Spice, we bring the soul of Maharashtra to your plate. Our recipes are passed down through generations, preserving the authentic Kolhapuri taste that food lovers crave.

Every dish is crafted in our 100% hygienic kitchen using the freshest ingredients and traditional spices sourced directly from Kolhapur. Our warm, family-friendly ambience makes every visit memorable.
